Day by Day

The Journey

Sun, Dec 24 San José → Monteverde Travel Day
  • 8:00am — Arrive San José Airport
  • Pick up rental car (Expedia/Enterprise)
  • Drive to Monteverde (~4hr)
  • 1:00pm — Arrive Monteverde · Check in El Establo Mountain Hotel
Mon, Dec 25 · Christmas Monteverde Adventure Day
  • 8:00am — SkyTrek, Sky Walk & TreeTram (self-guided)
  • 1:00pm — Arboreal Tree Climbing Park (2x kids)
  • Stay: El Establo Mountain Hotel
Tue, Dec 26 Monteverde → Arenal Wildlife Day
  • 11:00am — Arrive Arenal · Check in Magic Mountain Hotel
  • 3:00pm — Sloth Tour
Wed, Dec 27 Arenal Volcano Day
  • 12:00pm — Hike Arenal Volcano
  • Stay: Magic Mountain Hotel
Thu, Dec 28 Arenal → San José Travel Day
  • Drive to San José
  • Check in Costa Rica Marriott Hotel Hacienda Belen
Fri, Dec 29 San José → Quepos / Manuel Antonio Travel Day
  • Drive to Quepos (~3.5hr)
  • Check in Hotel La Mariposa · Premier Sunrise room
Sat, Dec 30 Manuel Antonio Park Day
  • 7:30am — Private Guided Tour · Manuel Antonio National Park
  • Beach & jungle exploration
  • Stay: Hotel La Mariposa
Sun, Dec 31 · New Year's Eve Manuel Antonio → Jacó (Herradura) NYE
  • Check out Hotel La Mariposa
  • Check in Los Sueños Marriott Ocean & Golf
  • Ring in 2024 🥂
Mon, Jan 1 · New Year's Day Jacó / Herradura Resort Day
  • Stay: Los Sueños Marriott Ocean & Golf
Tue, Jan 2 – Fri, Jan 5 Jacó / Herradura Vacation Club
  • Move to Marriott Vacation Club
  • Beach, pool & relaxation days
Sat, Jan 6 San José · Departure Fly Home
  • Drive to San José
  • Stay: Costa Rica Marriott Hotel Hacienda Belen
  • Return rental car · Fly home ✈️
